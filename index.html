A P ELEVATORS SERVICES is fastest growing Lift manufacturing, supplier and maintenance group in India. APES is scaling new horizons of achievement by catering to various needs of diverse commercial, housing and industrial sectors. APES has erected and maintains over hundreds of elevators in and around Delhi/NCR and different part of country. We have highly qualified, experienced technical staff under leadership Shri Ajay Pandey with 25 years domain expertise in the elevator industry. With standardized process and effective management we are able to provide prompt and efficient service to our customers 24 by 7 without any break or holiday. Customer satisfaction through timely, high quality deliverables and prompt after-sale-service make us distinct and has helped us to win hearts of many clients. Our major business is from referral & returning clients. We have no hesitation in claiming customer satisfaction is our main marketing tool which resulted into the continuously -growing list of our customers which is testimony of our success.
We have demonstrated history of supply, install & timely maintenance service to our customer as well as many elevators consulting group. The company undertakes all maintenance duties for the product which is supplied by us.

Annual Maintenance Contract

We offer annual maintenance & Preventive Maintenance (Non Comprehensive) service packages as per our customers’ needs. These services are offered at most competitive price to suit your requirements. Regular maintenance is not only mandatory as per government regulations but also essential for your own safety. AP Elevators Services is licensed elevator contractor by Government to maintain and install all types of lifts. Our AMCs package is designed to meet every customer’s needs in cost effective manner. Our trained and appropriately skilled personnel will regularly examine, lubricate and clean the machine, motor, controller, bearings, guides and if required make minor adjustments to ensure smooth and safe operation of elevator. We also provide routine checkups & emergency breakdown services along with AMCs.
